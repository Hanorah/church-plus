A brief story about how church plus was founded

HISTORY

Founded in 2011, CHURCH PLUS Benin liberates people from religious bondage through the gospel of grace. Starting with 37 members, predominantly students, the church fosters a dynamic worship environment promoting love, friendship, and excellence. With a vision to raise world leaders, the church invites all to join their journey of serving God with love and dedication.
